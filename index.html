About Sophie

Sophie Schnaper is originally from Orlando, Florida. She is a graduate student at University of Florida, where she is obtaining her Master’s degree in Digital Strategy and Web Design. Originally an elementary educator, she decided to change careers and further her education within the digital marketing and advertising sector. Sophie will graduate with her Master’s degree in August of 2023.

An extremely determined and self-motivated individual with experience in Branding and Marketing. Eager to extend her knowledge to get the job done. Previous experience has developed her ability to lead projects, organizations, and people. Known as an innovator, efficient worker, and fast learner - each skill has prepared Sophie to address the demands of competitive and evolving industry.

As both an analytical mind and creative, Sophie is interested in pursuing a career with a focus in Integrated Marketing.
